Acacia | Smart Irrigation

Acacia Irrigation has been one of my main projects at The Quadracci Sustainable Engineering Lab. Initially inspired by SharedSolar, another qSEL project, the focus is on finding a more economically feasible way to provide irrigation to smallholder farmers in the developing world. Initial pilots experimented with a single shared point of solar generation with no energy or water storage and a Pay As You Go (PAYGO) payment system. This decreased the initial capital cost for the installer while providing cleaner, more convenient, and cheaper water access for each connected farmer.
