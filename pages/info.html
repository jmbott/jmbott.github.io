Jack Bott | Engineer

Jack Bott is an Electrical and Mechanical Engineer, with a background building reliable hardware and software solutions for harsh environments, currently working at The Quadracci Sustainable Engineering Lab. The qSEL is a part of the Earth Institute at Columbia University, New York, NY. He has an MS in Electrical Engineering and a BS in Mechanical Engineering from Columbia University, as well as a BA in Physics from Bard College. Jack has field experience deploying control systems across Africa.
