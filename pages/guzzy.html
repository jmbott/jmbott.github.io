guzzy.ca | Artist Archive

This is a digital artist archive that I helped put together. It showcases some of the works of the late Paul de Guzman who always wanted to start an ad agency called Guzzy's.

My role in this project was to get the website working. My main focus was to display nearly two hundred photographs on a single mobile friendly page without overloading the user's device. To do this I used mainly vanilla JavaScript and a little jQuery. See the site linked below.
