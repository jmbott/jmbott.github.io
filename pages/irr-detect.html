Irrigation Detect | Computer Vision

Irrigation Detect is primarily a data collection project. It asks how can I collect meaningful ground truth data at a lower cost than surveying efforts? It explores using various sensors and street view cameras to do just that. With the GPS coordinates of your data capture vehicle, a concept of which direction your cameras are pointed in, and how far away objects in the frame are, you can geolocate features of interest frame by frame.
